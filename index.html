About us

At SSR Brick, we take pride in over a decade of expertise in brick manufacturing. Operating on a 2-acre facility with a dedicated team of more than 30 skilled laborers, we produce high-quality bricks known for their lightweight strength and durability. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name across North Karnataka and Telangana, ensuring timely delivery and superior craftsmanship. Customer satisfaction is our top priority. We strive to maintain zero complaints by consistently delivering the best. Choose SSR Brick for reliability, strength, and quality you can build on. We use advanced production techniques to maintain uniform quality in every brick. Our team continuously works towards innovation, ensuring our bricks meet the evolving needs of modern construction.
