Frequently Asked Questions

1. What products do you manufacture?

We produce high‑quality red clay bricks that meet IS 1077 standards for residential, commercial, and industrial construction.

2. Are your bricks tested for strength?

Yes. Every batch undergoes compressive‑strength and water‑absorption tests to ensure consistent quality.

3. Do you deliver outside Kalaburagi?

Yes, we deliver across Karnataka and Telangana. Delivery charges depend on distance and load size.

4. What is the minimum bulk order?

Our minimum bulk order is 1,000 bricks. Smaller sample orders can be arranged on request.

5. How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery is 1–2 business days and it's also depend on the distance as well.

6. Do you offer custom brick sizes?

Yes. We can manufacture custom dimensions for large projects, subject to a minimum order and lead time.

7. Are your bricks eco‑friendly?

We use locally sourced clay and energy‑efficient kilns, recycling heat where possible to reduce emissions.

8. Can I visit your factory?

Absolutely. Factory visits are always welcome.

9. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfer, UPI, and cash. A 30 % advance is required to confirm large orders.

10. Can you arrange unloading at site?

Yes. We can provide labour and equipment for unloading at an additional fee.

11. How should bricks be stored on site?

Keep bricks on level ground and stacked neatly.

12. Do you supply mortar or cement?

We specialise in bricks only. However, we can recommend trusted local suppliers for cement and mortar.

13. What happens if bricks arrive damaged?

We'll give some extra brick considering the damage brick. But we don't offer return or replacement option.

14. What brick varieties do you stock?

Classic Red, Sydney Grey, Brown Multi, Lightweight Hollow, and Custom mixes. See the “Types of Brick” section on our website.

15. Are sample bricks available?

Yes, up to 10 sample bricks per variety are free; courier charges apply outside Kalaburagi city.

16. How do I place an order?

Call +91 80501 31110, WhatsApp us via the “Contact” link, or email at ssrbrick@gmail.com with your quantity and delivery location or visit our site.
